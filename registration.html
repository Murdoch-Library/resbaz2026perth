Who can register?

✔️ Researchers at ALL levels and from ALL disciplines
✔️ PhD candidates, research masters students, and early career researchers
✔️ Research technologists and software engineers supporting research
✔️ All university or government-funded staff or students welcome!

Please note: commercial attendees are only permitted to attend through sponsorship agreements. Please get in touch for more details.

Cost of Living Support

The cost of living is making life very tough right now. We don't want anyone to miss out on ResBaz because of financial concerns, so please reach out for a free ticket if you are worried about the cost of attending ResBaz.

We are also offering free registration to volunteers and those that need it, so get in touch resbaz@qcif.edu.au if you need it or if you're keen to help out.
